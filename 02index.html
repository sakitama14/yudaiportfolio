= ("equals") is the fifth studio album[3][4] by English singer-songwriter Ed Sheeran, released on 29 October 2021 by Asylum and Atlantic Records.

The album was supported by four singles "Bad Habits", "Shivers", "Overpass Graffiti", and "The Joker and the Queen", and one promotional single, "Visiting Hours".

The album reached number one in Australia, Belgium, Canada, Denmark, France, Germany, Ireland, Italy, Lithuania, the Netherlands, New Zealand, Scotland, Sweden, the United Kingdom, and the United States.
